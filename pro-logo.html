Logo Design
This is a logo design for a product of my department in Meituan-Dianping. The product is named "Cloud Shop Assistant", which helps merchants manage their business on Meituan-Dianping app.
Design Keyword
The product "Cloud Shop Assistant" is a desktop app that is used by merchants to manage their business. Based on the product name and goal, we derived the following design keywords for the product's logo:
Efficiency
Open
Professional
Service
Intelligent
Cloud Shop
Brainstorming
We brainstormed the design concept based on the keywords above. Everyone come up with a few ideas about the logo design, and then we classify all the ideas into a coordinate axis with the x-axis being feasibility and y-axis being relevance to the theme.
After classification, we decided to take the concepts in the 2nd quadrant to design phase. Each of us took one concept. For me, I took the O2O(online to offline) concept.
Hand Drawings
This is my hand drawings of the concept O2O.
Design
This design derives from the concept of O2O(online to offline), which is exactly what our company is doing. Here are the meaning of this logo:
① It's a transformation of "O2O", meaning online to offline;
② The bottom part is like the number 8, which pronouces like "make a fortune" in Chinese, making it a great symbol for business people.
③ The whole shape of the logo is like a cloud, denoting the cloud shop concept of our product.
④ The color scheme derives from the color of our company: Green out of Meituan, and Yellow out of Dianping.
Another color version of this logo is white.
This white logo is more relevant to the concept of cloud.
