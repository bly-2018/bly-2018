eBay Journey 2016
eBay Journey 2016 is a website that shows a customer what he or she has done on eBay in 2016. It displays the user's GMB, GMV, first purchasing, first selling, etc., all in real data supported by eBay Data Service and Solutions (DSS) Department. I did the design and front-end development.
Brainstorming
The goal of 'eBay Journey' is to remind users their moments on eBay, and hopefully to bring them back to eBay in the future.

We can tell a story about their every purchase, or show them their behaviors in an interesting way.
Concept1--Time Machine

From keywords: Time, Travel, Sun.

  • A person is running towards the sun to take a time machine;
  • Click the 'launch' button to start the journey;
  • Experience your journey page by page, one story at a time;
Designed by me with Sketch
Concept2--Travelling

From keywords: Travel, Scenery, Story, Game.

  • A person is walking from 2016.1.1 to 2016.12.31;
  • He pauses if he did something on eBay that day;
  • What he did and what time he did it will show up during his pause;
  • He keeps walking after 3s pause, will stop again at next time point;
